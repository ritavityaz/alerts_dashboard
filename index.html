    About

    What is this?

    Missiles. Drones. Bombing. Trauma. No sleep. Nervous. Eat too much. Sad. Solution? Sleepy Lion will not help you. Must exercise. Take antidepressants. Stay sheltered. But at least you'll be able to quantify your suffering. Sleepy Lion will show you how many times your sleep was interrupted, so you can prove to your boss you're not lazy, just tired. And you can estimate the best times to take a shower without having to run naked to the MAMAD and scare the kids. YMMV. Stay safe, sleep tight, don't let the vibecode bugs bite. PS: translation not available in Farsi for obvious reasons.

    How to Use

    Use the map to explore alert density across Israel. Click a city or zone to drill down. Filter by threat type using the dropdown. The time slider lets you narrow the date range — use the arrow buttons to step through days or hours. The timeline shows alerts by time of day, and the heatmap highlights recurring peak hours.
